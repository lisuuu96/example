About Me

Hi! My name’s Oliwia. I’m a partly self-taught, partly art educated artist, born in Poland, raised in the UK, currently based in Switzerland, and a huuuuge fan of travels with my best friend of a husband. As to the art. I look for inspiration and draw from my surroundings wherever I am. Hence you don’t really decide when inspiration hits and what it is going to be, my works have no particular, distinct style. My media choice is just as random as my subject choice. And I love it. Not limiting oneself to a certain style and subject lets me appreciate all the details of the world around us. In all this chaos however, I am a perfectionist, so- as it always is- I am my harshest critic. Hope my art not will not only please the eye, but also help you notice the discreet beauty in unexpected places, just as I try to do.
